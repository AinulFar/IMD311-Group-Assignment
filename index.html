Story Telling
QUALITY FOOD DELICATENESS & MAGIC PLACE

Big Bite is a partnership business that was founded in 2022.
It is located in Jalan Yam Tuan, Seremban and will be launched
in 2023. Big Bite is a café that serves drinks, primarily coffee
and matcha, as well as desserts in a quick and easy manner.


Furthermore, we offer a variety of products for the drink
that allow people to choose their favourite, such as the
type of milk. We also provide a comfortable space for
people to hang out with their friends or do their work.
Other than that, we also want to do a seasonal menu so that
people can try different types of desserts and drinks.
